Build a polished, mobile-first web app called Petit Mokus for parents with young children.

The app should feel soft, playful, premium, and calming, with a child-friendly pastel aesthetic inspired by the Little Dutch brand style: gentle pastel tones, cozy nursery energy, rounded shapes, soft illustrations, airy spacing, and a warm, elegant feel rather than loud cartoon chaos.
 The design should feel modern and beautifully understated, with soft colors and playful details that appeal to both children and parents.

Overall concept
Petit Mokus is a multilingual listening app for small children with three tabs:

Sounds — the main tab, where children tap animals and hear the sound the animal makes.

Music — a tab with lullabies and folk songs in English, French, and Hungarian.

Stories — for now this tab is not implemented; only create the tab icon, placeholder card, and a “Coming soon” treatment themed around bedtime stories.

The app is for ages roughly 2–6 and should feel safe, magical, and very easy to use.
