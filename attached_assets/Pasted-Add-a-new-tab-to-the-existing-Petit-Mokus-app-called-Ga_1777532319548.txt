Gameplay ideas
Option A: Color matching
Show 3 to 5 large colored targets or baskets.

Show draggable or tappable items that must be matched to the correct color.

Example: drag the red apple to the red basket, or tap the yellow sun and then tap the yellow target.

Give positive feedback when matched correctly.

Option B: Shape matching
Show 3 to 5 large shape outlines or slots.

Show simple shapes such as circle, square, triangle, star.

Let children drag or tap-to-place the matching shape into the correct slot.

Give positive feedback for correct placement.
